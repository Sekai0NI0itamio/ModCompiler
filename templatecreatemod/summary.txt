Wanting to play as if your in a server? Tired of digging back up just to go back to your base? Well this mod cancels that pain, you can use /rtp, /tpa, /rtp just like if your in a server, but in single player!

Hate that creeper that doesn't burn after day? Hate the slimes that spawn in super flat worlds? This mob makes it so that ALL mobs burn in day.
