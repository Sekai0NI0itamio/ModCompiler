- Toggle on/off with K key (customizable keybind)
- In-game status messages (ON/OFF feedback)
- Dimension control (disabled in Nether by default)
- Fully configurable via config file
- Works with torches anywhere in inventory (not just hotbar)

- **Feedback**: Shows "Auto Torch Placer: ON" (green) or "OFF" (red)

## How It Works
1. Player moves through dark area with torches in inventory
2. Mod calculates average light level in 3x3x3 area around player
3. If average light < 7, searches for valid torch placement location
4. Prioritizes walls over ground for placement
5. Places torch and consumes one from inventory
6. Enforces minimum distance and cooldown to prevent spam
7. Checks every 2 ticks (0.1 seconds) when moving, 10 ticks when stationary
