# Old Notch Apples

Bring back the glory days of Minecraft 1.8.9! This mod makes Enchanted Golden Apples (Notch Apples) craftable again and restores their original powerful effects.

## Features

- ✅ **Craftable Notch Apples** - Craft with 8 gold blocks + 1 apple (just like the old days!)
- ✅ **1.8.9 Effects** - Restores the original powerful potion effects from Minecraft 1.8.9
- ✅ **Regeneration V** - Heal rapidly for 30 seconds
- ✅ **Absorption IV** - Extra health for 2 minutes
- ✅ **Resistance I** - Damage reduction for 5 minutes
- ✅ **Fire Resistance I** - Immunity to fire and lava for 5 minutes
- ✅ **Configurable** - Enable/disable features via config file

## Crafting Recipe

```
[Gold Block] [Gold Block] [Gold Block]
[Gold Block]   [Apple]    [Gold Block]
[Gold Block] [Gold Block] [Gold Block]
```

**8 Gold Blocks + 1 Apple = 1 Enchanted Golden Apple**

## Effects Comparison

### Vanilla 1.12.2 (Nerfed)
- Regeneration II for 20 seconds
- Absorption I for 2 minutes
- Resistance I for 5 minutes
- Fire Resistance I for 5 minutes

### This Mod (1.8.9 Restored)
- **Regeneration V** for 30 seconds ⚡
- **Absorption IV** for 2 minutes 💪
- **Resistance I** for 5 minutes 🛡️
- **Fire Resistance I** for 5 minutes 🔥

## Why This Mod?

In Minecraft 1.9+, Mojang removed the crafting recipe for Enchanted Golden Apples and nerfed their effects. This mod brings back the original 1.8.9 experience where Notch Apples were powerful, craftable items that rewarded players for their gold investment.

## Configuration

The mod creates a config file at `.minecraft/config/oldnotchapples.cfg`:

```
enabled=true              # Enable/disable the mod
use189Effects=true        # Use 1.8.9 effects (vs vanilla 1.12.2)
```

## Installation

1. Download the mod JAR file
2. Place it in your `.minecraft/mods` folder
3. Launch Minecraft 1.12.2 with Forge installed
4. Craft your Notch Apples and dominate!

## Compatibility

- **Server-side**: Required (handles crafting recipe and effects)
- **Client-side**: Optional (clients don't need the mod)
- **Mod Compatibility**: Works with all mods, no conflicts
