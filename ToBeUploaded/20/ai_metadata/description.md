# Auto Feeder

Tired of manually feeding animals for breeding? Auto Feeder does it for you!

## Features

🐄 **Automatic Feeding** - Animals are fed automatically from nearby chests
🎯 **Smart Pairing** - Only feeds animals in pairs to ensure breeding partners
⚡ **Efficient** - Checks chests when you interact with them and periodically
🔧 **Highly Configurable** - Control which animals to feed and timing

## How It Works

1. Place a chest near your animals (within 8 blocks)
2. Fill it with appropriate food (wheat for cows, carrots for pigs, etc.)
3. The mod automatically feeds animals in pairs when they're ready to breed
4. Watch your farm grow!

## Supported Animals

- **Cows** - Wheat
- **Sheep** - Wheat  
- **Pigs** - Carrot, Potato, Beetroot
- **Chickens** - Seeds (wheat, melon, pumpkin, beetroot)
- **Horses** - Golden Apple, Golden Carrot
- **Rabbits** - Carrot, Golden Carrot

## Smart Features

- **Pair-Based Feeding**: Only feeds even numbers of each animal type so every animal has a breeding partner
- **Efficient Checking**: Checks chests when you interact with them and periodically (configurable)
- **Per-Chest Cooldowns**: Prevents spam and respects breeding timers
- **Visual Feedback**: Heart particles and sounds when animals are fed

## Configuration

Edit `config/autofeeder.cfg` to customize:

- Enable/disable the mod
- Search range for animals (default: 8 blocks)
- Feeding interval (default: 100 ticks = 5 seconds)
- Enable/disable specific animal types
- Toggle sounds and particles
