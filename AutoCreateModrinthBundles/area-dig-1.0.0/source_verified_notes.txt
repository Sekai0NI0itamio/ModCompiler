GUARANTEED PLAYER-VISIBLE FACTS
- When you break a block with a pickaxe, axe, or shovel that has the area_dig enchantment, the mod breaks all blocks in a cube around that block.
- The cube size grows with the enchantment level (radius = level + 1).
- The broken blocks disappear instantly without particles or sound.
- All drops from the broken blocks appear at the block you originally broke.
- Each block broken reduces the tool's durability by 1.
- The enchantment only works on tools that can normally harvest the block (e.g., you cannot use it on a wooden pickaxe to break obsidian).
- The enchantment does not affect bedrock or air.
- The enchantment can be applied up to level 5.
