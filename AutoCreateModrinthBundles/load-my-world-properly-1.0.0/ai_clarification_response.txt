GUARANTEED PLAYER-VISIBLE FACTS
- Runs only on the client in singleplayer worlds.
- Waits for the world and player to be loaded before acting.
- Monitors for a loading screen or skipRenderWorld flag being true.
- After 10 world‑ready ticks and 40 stuck ticks it triggers a fix.
- The fix clears the skipRenderWorld flag.
- It removes any active GUI screens such as download terrain or working screens.
- It reloads renderers if they exist.
- It sets the render view entity to the player.
- It sets the ingame focus.

DO NOT CLAIM- It may not trigger if the tick thresholds are not reached.
- It may not work if the player leaves the world before the condition is met.
- It does not add new items, blocks, mobs, or commands (not shown).
- It does not modify game mechanics such as mob spawning or item drops (not shown).
