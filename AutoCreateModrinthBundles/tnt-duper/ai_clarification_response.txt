UARANTEED PLAYER-VISIBLE FACTS
- Dispensers that contain TNT will spawn a primed TNT entity when activated.
- The spawned TNT behaves like normal TNT and can be picked up or detonated.
- The TNT item used to trigger the dispenser is not consumed, so it remains in the dispenser.
- The dispenser only plays the TNT priming sound; no other dispensing animation occurs.
