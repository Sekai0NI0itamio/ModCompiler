GUARANTEED PLAYER-VISIBLE FACTS
- Breaking a fully grown wheat, carrot, potato, or beetroot block can cause the mod to place the same crop block back after a short delay, using one seed from the player's inventory.
- Breaking a fully grown cocoa bean block (age 2 or more) can cause the mod to plant a new cocoa bean block in the same spot.
- The replanting happens on the server side and only if the block space is empty at that time.
- The delay used is about 1 tick (≈50 ms).
- The player does not need to place seeds manually; the mod consumes the seed automatically.

DO NOT CLAIM
- The mod does not add any new items, blocks, or commands.
- Tree replanting behavior is not detailed in the source.
- The exact outcome of cocoa replanting beyond the age check is unknown.
- The mod does not guarantee replanting for all crop types (e.g., melons, pumpkins are not handled).
- The replanting may not occur if the player has no seed or if the block is not air at the scheduled time.
- The mod works only on the server side; client‑side effects are not described.
- No visual or sound effects are mentioned in the source.
- The mod does not change block drop behavior or mob drops.
- The mod does not require any player configuration or configuration files.
- The mod does not affect growth speed or bone‑meal usage.
